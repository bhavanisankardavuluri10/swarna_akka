Our Beautiful Story

From the very first Rakhi you tied on my wrist, life felt different — brighter, warmer, and filled with meaning. That day, I didn’t just receive a thread; I received the most precious sister anyone could ever dream of. From that magical moment, you became my medicine — not just healing my wounds, but filling every corner of my life with love, warmth, and endless care.

Our lockdown days felt like chapters from a magical story — evenings spent playing shuttle under the open sky, endless hours simply being together, and moments that still sparkle in my heart like stars. When we moved closer to each other, it was like finding home. From your heavenly biryani that I can never get enough of, to our shared love for chicken and Thumbs Up, every little thing with you feels special. And how could I ever forget our first trip to Vizag with your amazing colleagues? Every adventure with you has painted my life in the brightest colors of joy.

You’re not just my sister — you’re my confidante who guards all my secrets, my biggest cheerleader who celebrates every success, and my inspiration who never stops, even when you’re unwell. Your gentle words, endless care, and radiant joy are the precious gifts that make you truly extraordinary.
